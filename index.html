Biography

I'm currently a fifth-year Ph.D student at the College of Computer Science and Technology at Zhejiang University, fortunately advised by Prof. Jian Wu, and scheduled to graduate in 2025. I got my Master's degree from Hunan University in July, 2020 majoring in Computer Science(CS), College of Computer Science and Electronic Engineering(CSEE), fortunately advised by Prof. Xin Liao. I got my Bachelor's degree from Hunan University in July, 2017 majoring in Software Engineering(CS), College of Computer Science and Electronic Engineering(CSEE). Before I got to Hunan University, all my life was spent in my hometown, Huining, Gansu province.

Research Interests

AI for Science, Computer Vision, and Multimedia Security. I focus on Computer Vision methods and their applications for Sciences, including knowledge distillation and fusion for image classification and segmentation.
